About us

The Robotics Club, IISER Mohali is a student run group of highly motivated undergraduate and graduate students from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Mohali. We believe science and technology should go hand in hand aimed at innovation and recreation of interesting technologies to be used in robots and it's research. Our mission is to push forward what's possible as a student organisation, both in terms of technical accomplishments and educational value. That is why we welcome students from all backgrounds and experiences, and offer them real hands on experience in constructing robots and leading a team. Our ultimate goal is to open the possibility of becoming a robotics innovator to anyone. We achieve it via workshop sessions, exhibitions and seminars by experts working worldwide in this field.
